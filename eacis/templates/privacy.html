DATA PROTECTION

Privacy Policy

How we collect, protect, and manage your personal data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

1. Data Collection Core Principles

E-ACIS operates on three core principles as mandated by the National Privacy Commission (NPC):

  • Transparency: We inform you exactly what data is collected.
  • Legitimate Purpose: We only collect data necessary for marketplace functions.
  • Proportionality: We avoid excessive or unnecessary data collection.

2. Personal Information Collected

We collect and process the following types of data:

  • Personal Identity: Full name, contact number, and email.
  • Merchant Data: Business names and regulatory permits.
  • Transactional Data: Order history and delivery addresses.
  • System Logs: Audit logs for security monitoring and compliance.

3. Rights of Data Subjects

Under RA 10173, you have the following rights reaching your personal data:

  • Right to be informed of data processing.
  • Right to access and correction of your data.
  • Right to erasure or blocking (deletion).
  • Right to object to processing.

4. Data Retention & Security

Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases (AES-256). We retain data only for the period necessary to fulfill service requirements or as required by Philippine law. Regular security audits are performed to maintain NPC Circular 16-01 compliance.

5. Contact Information

For concerns regarding your data privacy, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO). You may also refer to the National Privacy Commission for regulatory guidelines.
