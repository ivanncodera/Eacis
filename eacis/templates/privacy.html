Privacy Policy

How we collect, protect, and manage your personal data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

E-ACIS Privacy Policy

Document date: April 18, 2026

1. Privacy Principles

E-ACIS processes data according to transparency, legitimate purpose, and proportionality. We collect only what is reasonably necessary to operate marketplace workflows, support requests, return validations, and account security.

  • Transparency: We inform you exactly what data is collected.
  • Legitimate Purpose: We only collect data necessary for marketplace functions.
  • Proportionality: We avoid excessive or unnecessary data collection.

2. Data We Collect

Depending on the feature you use, E-ACIS may process the following categories:

  • Account and Profile Data: name, email, phone, role, and address details.
  • Commerce Data: cart, orders, payments, refunds, vouchers, and loyalty transactions.
  • Support and Resolution Data: inquiry tickets, return reasons, descriptions, and evidence links/uploads submitted for claims.
  • Seller Compliance Data: seller business profile and permit documentation for verification workflows.
  • Security and Audit Data: access logs, action traces, and other records used for abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and compliance review.

3. Why We Process Your Data

We process personal data to operate and secure the platform, including:

  • Account authentication, session handling, and role-based access.
  • Order processing, shipping coordination, and seller-customer communication.
  • Installment and eligibility workflows where applicable.
  • Return and refund validation, including fraud and abuse detection controls.
  • Operational reporting, administrative audits, and legal/compliance obligations.

4. Sharing, Retention, and Safeguards

Data may be shared only as needed to deliver services and meet legal obligations, such as with relevant counterparties in a transaction, authorized service providers, or competent authorities when required by law.

  • Retention: Records are retained only as long as needed for support, transaction integrity, dispute handling, and compliance.
  • Safeguards: Access controls, security configuration, and auditability are applied to protect system and user data.
  • Evidence Files: Return evidence is processed for claim verification, fraud prevention, and case resolution workflows.

5. Data Subject Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, objection, restriction, or deletion of your personal data. You may also request information on how your data is processed and raise privacy concerns through official support channels.

6. Data Protection Officer & Contact

For privacy requests, questions, or complaints, contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@e-acis.ph. You may also open a ticket in the Support Center.

7. Breach Notification and Incident Response

We maintain an incident response program. In the event of a personal data breach affecting user data, we will notify affected individuals and the National Privacy Commission when required by law, and take measures to contain and remediate the breach.

8. Retention and Records of Processing

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specific retention periods vary by data category; contact the DPO for the retention schedule and Records of Processing Activities (RoPA).

9. Third-Party Processors & International Transfers

We share data with service providers (payment processors, couriers, analytics). Where data is transferred internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards (contractual clauses, provider assurances). A list of major processors is available from the DPO upon request.

10. Children and Sensitive Data

The platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent. Sensitive categories of data are processed only when strictly necessary for verification or compliance, and with safeguards.

11. Automated Decisions and Profiling

We use automated systems for fraud detection, eligibility checks, and personalization. Such processing includes human oversight and review; users may request review or object to automated decisions via the Support Center.

12. Contact and Policy Updates

For privacy concerns, contact E-ACIS support at support@e-acis.ph or the DPO at dpo@e-acis.ph. You may also consult the National Privacy Commission. We may revise this policy when platform features or legal requirements change.
