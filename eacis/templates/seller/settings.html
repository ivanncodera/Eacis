Seller FAQ

Quick answers to common seller questions — concise and actionable.

How do I update my business profile?
Click the Business Profile card above to edit your business name, contact details, and address. Save changes at the bottom of the form.
When are payouts sent?
Payouts are scheduled weekly on the next Friday and reflect orders that reached delivered status and cleared any hold periods. See Payouts for details.
How do I change my account password or 2FA?
Use the Security card to change your password and configure two-factor protections. If you lose access, contact support immediately.
How many product images can I upload?
You may upload up to 3 images per product. Use the seller product editor to manage images and their order.
