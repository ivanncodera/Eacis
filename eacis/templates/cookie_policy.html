E-ACIS Cookie Policy

Document date: April 18, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to remember preferences, sessions, and to enable analytics and advertising features. We also use similar technologies like local storage and pixel tags.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required to support login, session management, shopping cart, and security features.
  • Performance: Anonymous cookies for analytics and product performance measurement.
  • Functional: Remember your preferences, language, and UI choices.
  • Advertising: Third-party cookies used to deliver relevant offers and measure campaign effectiveness.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings and privacy controls. Note that disabling essential cookies may break core functionality like authentication and checkout. For third-party cookie opt-outs, consult provider privacy notices.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We integrate services (analytics, ads, payment providers, courier tracking) that may set cookies on their domains. These providers control their cookies and you should consult their policies for opt-out choices.

5. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy contact dpo@e-acis.ph or visit the Support Center.
