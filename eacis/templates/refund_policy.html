Refunds & Returns

Document date: April 18, 2026

1. Eligibility

Return eligibility depends on the seller's configured return window, product condition, delivery confirmation, and the reason for return. Some items (e.g., custom, hazardous) may be non-returnable. Check product listing for seller-specific terms.

2. How to Request a Return

Open a return request from your orders page, provide required evidence (photos, descriptions), and follow instructions for pickup or drop-off. Sellers and couriers coordinate the return logistics.

3. Evidence and Fraud Prevention

We may require photos, video, or other evidence to verify claims. Excessive or fraudulent return behavior may result in account restrictions. Evidence is processed for claim verification and fraud prevention.

4. Refund Timing and Methods

Refunds are processed once return acceptance is confirmed. Timing depends on payment provider and may take several business days. Refunds are made to the original payment method unless otherwise agreed.

5. Contact and Escalation

If you have questions or disputes about returns or refunds, contact Support via the Support Center. Escalations can be directed to support@e-acis.ph.
