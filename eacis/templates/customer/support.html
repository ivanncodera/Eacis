Frequently Asked Questions

What is the E-ACIS return policy?
Our standard return policy allows for returns within 7 days of delivery for eligible items. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and tags intact.
How do installment payments work?
E-ACIS offers flexible installment plans for eligible customers. You can pay over 3, 6, or 12 months depending on the seller's configuration. Due dates and plan status are tracked in your Installment Center, and posted payments appear once your seller confirms settlement.
How do I earn loyalty points?
You earn 1 point for every PHP 100 spent on completed orders. Points can be used to get discounts on your future purchases during checkout.

Track order status from Pending to Delivered

Returns 4 min read

Submit returns and monitor refund progression

Create protection claims, keep your return reference, and follow status changes until final refund completion.

  • Start a claim: provide order ref, photos of item and packaging, and a short description of the issue.
  • Keep your RRT: the return reference (RRT) is required for tracking and refund verification.
  • Refund timing: once approved, refunds may take 3–7 business days depending on payment method.

Create targeted tickets, include complete issue details, and track responses from assigned sellers faster.
